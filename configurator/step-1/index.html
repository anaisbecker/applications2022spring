Configurator: Research

Starbucks App

  • Initial Observations:
  • Start off with sizing using buttons (visual rep. Of sizes)
  • Shows whats automatically included + customize button
  • Sections for espresso, sweetener, flavor, toppings, add-ins
  • Liked:
  • Liked the visuals (drawings of coffee cups)
  • Very simple, not too overwhelming
  • Clearly shows ingredients/nutrition
  • Didn’t Like:
  • I wish there was an easier drop down way to see things to add in
  • Wish there were more visuals in customization/configuration

McDonalds App

  • Initial Observations:
  • Clearly shows sizing options, then quantity
  • Can click customize
  • Change what’s included, then add it on
  • Drop down with quantities
  • Liked:
  • Liked that there were visuals within the customization (pics of sugar etc)
  • Almost even simpler than Starbucks
  • Didn’t like:
  • When you change the size, it looks like the picture will change to match (doesnt)
  • Customize button is a lot smaller than Starbucks

Target/Common Audience

  • More often younger people use apps (as they tend to be more tech savvy)
  • However, an app that is more user friendly could allow the older generations to also become a target audience

Thoughts on Direction

  • Curious about the idea of soda/red bull drinks instead of iced coffee (more interesting in configuration)
  • Customize button LARGE
  • Visuals that accompany configuration (at least somewhat, even with sizes)
  • Thinking of going more toward Figma style (not HTML), in order to get better visual prototyping
